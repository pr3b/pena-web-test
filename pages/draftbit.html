The Company:

The Chicago-based company Draftbit provides the quickest, most efficient, and least complicated method for creating native mobile apps. They collaborated with Pena to create user manuals as part of their effort to improve usability.

Pena improved the clarity and provided direct support for Draftbit's native mobile app by creating several user manuals.
