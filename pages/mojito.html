What we did:

API & Product Management

The Company:

Mojito, a global NFT software platform, prioritizes usability and accessibility for customers. With their rapidly expanding community and tokenized consumer engagement, their goal is to launch and grow NFT marketplaces and bring blockchain to the masses.

The Approach:

To maximize their ability to develop new NFT marketplaces, Mojito needed our assistance with API documentation and minutes of their weekly meetings. Users must first have access to everything without scouring the site for technical support in order to be able to sell NFTs from their own storefronts and websites, with this goal in mind, Pena helped them promote their accessible and user-friendly navigable platform via API documentation and product support.

The Results:

Our API documentation has allowed industry-leading brands and IP holders to continue taking advantage of the present and future of cryptocurrency, further allowing Mojito to excel as a company and continue helping in the development of Sotheby's new digital NFT marketplace.
